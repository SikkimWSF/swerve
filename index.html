About

In the serene valleys of Sikkim, where the mighty Himalayas watches over fields of rice and blooming rhododendrons, the Sikkim WESEAN Regional Student Voice for Empowerment rises like a fresh mountain breeze. Rooted in the rich traditions of this land, where prayer flags flutter in the crisp air and ancient monasteries hold centuries of wisdom, students from every corner of Sikkim unite with a shared purpose.
